How to use PacketGuard

Getting Started

Go to the Dashboard, upload a CSV of network traffic in NSL-KDD format (41 feature columns, no header row), and click Analyze Traffic. Results appear as a risk gauge, category breakdown, and per-category confidence.

Attack Categories

NormalLegitimate traffic, no attack detected
DoSDenial of Service — flooding to overwhelm a target
ProbeReconnaissance/scanning — mapping the network before an attack
R2LRemote to Local — unauthorized access attempt from outside
U2RUser to Root — privilege escalation on an already-accessed system
